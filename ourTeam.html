OUR TEAM

McMaster Hyperloop is comprised of 50 ambitious students, all working towards designing and building a functional prototype which we will be taking to compete at the SpaceX Hyperloop Pod Competition. We are striving to include all fields of research including biology, engineering, and economics, bringing a unique, unified approach to the international competition.
